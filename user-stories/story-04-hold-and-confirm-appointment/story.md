# User Story 4

## User Story
As a patient, I want to hold and confirm an appointment slot so that I can complete a booking.

## Story Points
8 story points

## Acceptance Criteria
### Patient
- Patients should be able to place a temporary hold on an available appointment slot before confirming the booking.
- A held slot should not be available for other patients while the hold is active.
- The system should clearly show how long the hold remains valid before it expires.
- Patients should be able to confirm the held slot to complete the appointment booking.
- If the hold expires before confirmation, the slot should be released and made available again.
- Patients should receive a clear booking confirmation once the appointment has been successfully confirmed.
