# User Story 10

## User Story
As a patient, I want to cancel an appointment so that my schedule stays accurate.

## Story Points
3 story points

## Acceptance Criteria
### Patient
- Patients should be able to cancel an eligible upcoming appointment from their appointment list or appointment details page.
- The system should ask patients to confirm the cancellation before the appointment is cancelled.
- Once cancelled, the appointment status should update clearly in the portal.
- The cancelled slot should become available again if the appointment falls within the allowed cancellation policy.
- Patients should receive confirmation that the appointment has been cancelled successfully.
