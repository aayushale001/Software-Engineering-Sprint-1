# User Story 12

## User Story
As a patient, I want to manage my medical record entries so that I can keep my health information updated.

## Story Points
8 story points

## Acceptance Criteria
### Patient
- Patients should be able to add new personal medical record entries, such as allergies, medications, or conditions.
- Patients should be able to edit existing personal medical record entries to keep them accurate and up to date.
- Patients should be able to delete a personal medical record entry after confirming the action.
- Required fields should be validated before a medical record entry can be saved.
- Changes to medical record entries should be reflected in the portal immediately after they are saved.
