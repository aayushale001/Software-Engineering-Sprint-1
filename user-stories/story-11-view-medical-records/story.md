# User Story 11

## User Story
As a patient, I want to view my medical records so that I can review my health information online.

## Story Points
5 story points

## Acceptance Criteria
### Patient
- Patients should be able to access their medical records only after signing in securely.
- Patients should be able to view a list of their medical record entries in the portal.
- Patients should be able to open a medical record entry to view its details, such as notes, dates, and related health information.
- The portal should show medical records in a clear and readable format.
- Patients should only be able to view their own medical records and not the records of other patients.
